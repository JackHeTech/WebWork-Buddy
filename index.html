Please note:

  1. You can type

    \sqrt(

    for square root (alternatively, you can just set your expression to the power of 1 / 2

  2. For multiplication, there is no need to type

    *


  3. Never have the sequence of characters

    cdot

    anywhere in your expression. This is because my parsing algorithm filters them out entirely to avoid confusion with other common WebWork / latex internal symbols.
